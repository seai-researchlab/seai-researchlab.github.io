Opportunities at SE+AI Research Lab

  • We are actively looking for highly motivated PhD and MSc students (who has demonastrated passion for research).
  • Requirements for PhD applicants:
    • General: A good academic standing in MSc (no C or equivalent grades in any CS courses) with at least one published article(s)/paper(s) in the area of software engineering.
    • Technical:
  • Requirements for MSc applicants:
    • General: A good academic standing in BSc (no C or equivalent grades in any CS courses) with at least one submitted/published article(s)/paper(s) in the area of software engineering.
    • Technical:
  • What you need to send for your application: (1) A detailed CV, (2) Transcripts, (3) A Statement of Purpose or Cover Letter, (4) One sample technical writing or published article/paper, (5) A research proposal of max 2-page, (6) IELTS Score (at least band 6.5 in all areas and a band of 7 overall).
  • In your Statement of Purpose or Cover Letter, you MUST explain and demonstrate (with evidence) your passion for research.
  • You MUST INCLUDE your IELTS or English Test score with your application.
  • If you are interested in SE and AI research, please send me your detailed CV at francis.palma@unb.ca with a subject line "Potential PhD Applicant" or "Potential MSc Applicant". We will contact you if you are a good match.
